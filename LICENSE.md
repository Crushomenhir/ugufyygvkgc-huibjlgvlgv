## › FAQ

**Q: Does it work with all games?**  
A: It works with most DirectX, OpenGL, and Vulkan titles running in windowed or borderless modes. Some exclusive fullscreen games may require borderless window instead.

**Q: Will this improve my FPS?**  
A: No. Lossless Scaling does not increase actual frame rate. It can make motion appear smoother through frame generation, but rendering performance depends on the original application.

**Q: Is frame generation suitable for competitive gaming?**  
A: Frame generation adds a small amount of latency. It is best suited for single-player or casual games where visual smoothness matters more than minimal input delay.

**Q: Can I use it on a 4K or ultrawide monitor?**  
A: Yes. The tool is designed specifically for high-resolution displays and works well with any modern monitor resolution.

**Q: Does it require administrator rights?**  
A: No administrator privileges are needed. The application runs as a standard user.
