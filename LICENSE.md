lossless scaling is a small desktop app that resizes any windowed
content to fit your screen while keeping visual clarity intact. It’s
built for games, emulators, media players, and older software that don’t
scale well on modern displays. Instead of stretching pixels blindly, it
applies smart scaling methods that preserve sharp edges and readable
text. You run it when you need it, close it when you’re done, and it
stays out of your way.

✦ Features

-   Pixel-perfect scaling — upscale low-resolution content without blur
    or distortion
-   Multiple scaling modes — choose between nearest, integer, and
    adaptive algorithms
-   Hotkey control — toggle scaling instantly without leaving your app
-   Window detection — automatically hooks into the active window
-   Borderless support — works with borderless and classic windowed apps
-   Low overhead — minimal CPU and GPU usage during operation
-   Portable build — no install, no registry traces

› FAQ

Q: Does it work with all games?
A: Most windowed and borderless apps work fine. Exclusive fullscreen is
not supported.

Q: Will this affect performance?
A: The impact is minimal. On modern systems it’s barely noticeable.

Q: Can I switch scaling modes on the fly?
A: Yes, use hotkeys to change modes without restarting anything.

Q: Does it modify the original application?
A: No. It only adjusts how the window is displayed on your screen.

Q: Is installation required?
A: No. It’s fully portable and runs as-is.
